Tada Subscription

  • You can subscribe for unlimited access to our full catalog and premium content
  • Get unlimited access to all Tada pictures and color picture you want.
  • The subscription is $3.99 a month with a week free trial
  • When you confirm your subscription a payment will be charged to your iTunes account.
  • The subscription renews automatically unless auto-renewal is turned off for at least 24-hours before the end of the current period
  • Subscriptions can be canceled at any time and you will still have access to unlimited subscriptions till your subscription date expires.
  • The account will be charged for renewal within 24-hours prior to the end of the current period at the cost of the chosen package.
  • Subscriptions may be managed by the user and auto-renewal may be turned off by going to the user's Account Settings after purchase.
